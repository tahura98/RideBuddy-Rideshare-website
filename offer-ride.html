Tips for safe ride offers

Only share rides with verified IUB students.

•Pick a well-lit, safe pickup spot near campus.
•Confirm passenger details before starting the ride.
•Keep emergency contacts saved & phone charged.
•Update available seats if someone cancels.
•Be respectful and follow campus guidelines.
